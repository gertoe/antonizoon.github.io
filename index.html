Archivis.me is the personal website of Antonizoon (Lawrence Wu), a renegade archivist, self-taught programmer, and head archivist at the Bibliotheca Anonoma.

Topics on this website range from a personal profile, programming/modding projects (and mishaps), device/game/show reviews, and philosophical tripe.

Topics concerning the Bibliotheca Anonoma can be found at the Official BA Blog.

Topics concerning the archival efforts of the Bibliotheca Anonoma Software Liquidation Commission (BASLQC) can be found at the BASLQC Blog.
